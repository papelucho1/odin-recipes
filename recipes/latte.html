Latte

Description

Caffè latte, often shortened to just latte in English, is a coffee beverage of Italian origin made with espresso and steamed milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1⅓ cups hot freshly brewed dark roast espresso coffee

Steps

  1. Heat milk in a saucepan set over medium-low heat. Whisk briskly with a wire whisk to create foam.
  2. Brew espresso and pour into four cups. Pour in milk, holding back the foam with a spoon. Spoon foam over the top.
